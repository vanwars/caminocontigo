Community Colleges

  • Community Colleges degrees range from certification and/or diplomas to associate degrees. An associate degree can be used for career advancement or for transferring to a four-year college. Four-year colleges offer bachelor’s degrees and, depending on the college, master’s and doctorate degrees. It is important to know which degree type is preferred for your future professional objective.
  • Classes are cheaper and admission is much easier. Many community colleges have agreements with four-year colleges, so that if you decide college is right for you, you can then transfer those credits.
